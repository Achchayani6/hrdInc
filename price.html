PARSING MODULE

  • Automated Data Extraction:

    Seamlessly extracts relevant information from resumes and applications, includes Contact details, Education and qualifications, Work experience, Skills and certifications, Keywords and other relevant data

  • Consistent Formatting:

    Converts extracted data into a structured format, regardless of the original file type, ensuring easy organization and analysis.

  • Database Integration:

    Stores parsed data directly into ARSSS's central database, making it accessible for matching, searching, and analysis.

  • Handles Multiple Formats:

    Processes resumes and applications in various formats, including PDF, Word, TXT, HTML, and more, eliminating compatibility issues

  • Customizable Fields:

    Allows users to define specific data fields to be extracted, tailoring the parsing process to their unique needs and requirements.

MATCHING MODULE

  • Smart Algorithm:

    Utilizes an intelligent algorithm to match candidates to open positions based on a comprehensive set of criteria, includes Skills and experience, Qualifications, Education and certifications, Keywords, Location preferences, Salary expectations and Company culture fit.

  • Customizable Weightage:

    Allows users to assign different weights to certain criteria based on their hiring priorities, tailoring the matching process to specific needs.

  • Resume Ranking:

    Generates a ranked list of candidates for each position, with the most relevant candidates appearing at the top, streamlining the selection process.

  • Compatibility Assessment:

    Goes beyond skills and experience, considering factors like company culture fit and salary expectations to ensure long-term success.

ARSSS:AUTOMATED RECRUITEMENT & STAFFING SOLUTION SOFTWARE

  • Matching Module:

    Matches candidates to open positions based on skills, experience, and fit, saving time and boosting hiring accuracy

  • Parsing Module:

    Extracts data from resumes and applications, eliminating manual data entry and ensuring consistent formatting.

  • Data Management Tools:

    Organizes candidate information, tracks applications, and facilitates communication, streamlining the entire recruitment process.

  • Advanced Features:

    Heat maps for data visualization, resume ranking based on custom weightage, and multi-format file compatibility.
